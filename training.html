Astronaut Preparation Training

"Astronaut Candidates are required to complete military water survival prior to beginning their flying syllabus, and become SCUBA qualified to prepare them for the extravehicular activity training.

They will also have to take language classes and you will need to be able to talk with the Russian Mission Control Center" (Blodgett)
